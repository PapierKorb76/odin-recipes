Fiorentina Steak Recipe

Description

This recipe is about a Fiorentina Steak, very few ingredients are needed to taste this fantastic dish

This steak is made from a high-quality piece of meat


Ingredients

  • 1 Fiorentina Steak (1kg)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. A couple hours before cooking the meat you should put it out of the fridge
  2. Put the steak on a baking sheet, make the grill warm up and put the meat on it, cook it for like 5 minutes without flipping it and check it during the whole process
  3. Now flip your steak and do the 2 step again, then make it rest for like 5 minutes and finally put some salt and pepper on your meat
  4. Now you can taste your steak, bon appetit!
